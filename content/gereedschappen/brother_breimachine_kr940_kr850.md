## Specificaties
* 4.5 Gauge (mm)	
* 200 Needles
* Yarns: Lace weight to DK weight
* Patterning method: built in computerized patterning
* Patterning Types: Fair Isle, Single Motif, Tuck Stitch, Skip (Slip) Stitch, Thread (Punch) Lace, Weaving, Plating, Intarsia
* bouwjaar 1988
